What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to launch your NLP products

Chatbot Development

Chatbot development is a rapidly growing field that focuses on creating intelligent conversational agents capable of interacting with users in a natural and engaging manner. Chatbots leverage Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and machine learning algorithms to understand and respond to user queries, providing automated assistance and support across various industries.

Text Analytics

Text analytics, also known as text mining or text data mining, is a powerful approach that involves extracting valuable insights, patterns, and knowledge from unstructured textual data. It encompasses a range of techniques, including natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, and statistical analysis, to derive meaningful information from vast amounts of text.

Language Generation

Language generation is an exciting field within natural language processing (NLP) that focuses on creating human-like text or speech using computational models. It involves generating coherent and contextually appropriate sentences, paragraphs, or even entire documents based on given prompts or patterns.

App Deployement

The deployment of Natural Language Processing (NLP) applications is a crucial step in bringing the power of these technologies into real-world scenarios. NLP deployment involves the process of taking trained models and making them accessible and operational within production environments.

Text Mining

Text mining is a powerful technique that involves extracting valuable insights, patterns, and knowledge from large volumes of unstructured text data. With the exponential growth of digital information, text mining has become essential for businesses and researchers to gain a deeper understanding of textual content and uncover hidden trends.

Text Classification and Clustering

Text classification and clustering are fundamental techniques in natural language processing (NLP) that enable the organization and analysis of textual data. These approaches play a crucial role in various applications, from information retrieval to sentiment analysis and content recommendation systems.

Recent Works

Check out some of our latest publications

Regret

Text Classification

We present a novel dataset of Reddit texts that have been classified into three classes: Regret by Action, Regret by Inaction, and No Regret.

Irony

Social Media Profiling

Irony profiling can help in identifying stereotype spreaders and can enhance the understanding of author behaviours. We proposed a methodology focusing on feature engineering to classify irony for long texts based on multiple linguistic and emotion-based features.

Hope

Text classification

This paper presents a hope speech dataset that classifies each tweet first into “Hope" and “Not Hope", then into three fine-grained hope categories: “Generalized Hope", “Realistic Hope", and “Unrealistic Hope" (along with “Not Hope").

Rumour

Text classification

We compared the rumour and non-rumour source tweets and then their corresponding reply (response) tweets to understand how they differ linguistically.

Social media question answering

Question Answering

The paper tackles the problem of question answering on social media data through an extractive approach.

Multi-label emotions

Low resource languages

We created the first multi-label emotion dataset consisting of 6,043 tweets and six basic emotions in the Urdu Nastalíq script. A multi-label (ML) classification approach was adopted to detect emotions from Urdu

Sexism in text

Text classification

The paper presents best performing machine learning and deep learning algorithms as well as BERT results on “sEXism Identification in Social neTworks (EXIST 2021)” shared task.
